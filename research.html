Flat Cap Co. – Sourcing and Product Design

Example 4: Research and Design Using AI – Camo Mesh Sourcing

Step 1: Research Camo Mesh Suppliers

Start with a conversation that identifies suitable mesh materials for hat production:

Can you help me identify 5 suppliers of camo-pattern mesh material suitable for hats? Prioritize suppliers that ship to the U.S. and offer samples.

ChatGPT will suggest suppliers, product types, or considerations. Ask for eco-friendly options or minimum order quantities as needed.

Step 2: Create a Comparison Table

Once you have a list of suppliers, ask for a comparison chart:

Can you compare these suppliers in a table with columns for: Supplier Name, Mesh Type, Camo Variety, MOQ, Lead Time, and Location?

Use this table to clarify strengths and limitations across vendors.

Step 3: Build a Decision Tree

Ask ChatGPT to help you visualize your decision-making logic:

Create a decision tree to help us choose between these suppliers based on lead time, customization, and MOQ.

Revise the flow to include factors like U.S. location or pricing as needed.

Bonus: Design a New Flat Cap Using the Selected Material

Now that you’ve researched materials, you can shift to design. Tell ChatGPT:

Based on the camo mesh materials we’ve just reviewed, can you help me design a new flat cap style that uses one of these materials as the base? Include details like color placement, panel structure, and inner lining.

Follow up by refining the design:

  • “Make it more summer-appropriate.”
  • “Use black/grey camo instead of green.”
  • “What kind of closure system would match this aesthetic?”

Then, generate a visual concept:

Can you create an image of this flat cap design?

ChatGPT will produce an image you can use as a concept mockup.

Key Takeaways

  • Use AI to research options faster and compare them clearly.
  • Request decision trees or comparison tools to support purchasing choices.
  • Use research output as input to design innovative new products.
  • AI supports not just sourcing, but product development workflows too.
