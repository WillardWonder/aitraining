Flat Cap Co. Email Response Workshop

This activity helps you train ChatGPT to match your personal writing style. You'll work through 3 realistic emails—from a coworker, a boss, and a customer—and progressively teach the AI to sound more like you.

How to Use This Activity

  1. Copy each email and paste it into ChatGPT.
  2. Use the starter prompt to generate a draft response.
  3. Say: "Actually, I would say something like this..." and type your version.
  4. Let ChatGPT adjust to match your voice.
  5. By the third example, the AI should require fewer changes.
  6. Finally, use: "Based on my edits, create a writing profile that matches my style."

Email #1 – Casual Request from a Coworker

Email from Jordan L. <jordan.l@flatcapco.com>
From: Jordan L. <jordan.l@flatcapco.com> To: You Sent: Wednesday, 10:04 AM Subject: Quick question! Hey! For the team-building day next week—and the potluck part—what were you bringing again? I know we talked about it at some point but I totally blanked. Just trying to get the list together today. Let me know when you have a sec! Thanks!! —Jordan
Prompt: "Write a casual but clear reply confirming what I’m bringing, and keep the tone friendly and relaxed."
Follow-up: "Actually, I would say something like this..." (type your own version)

Email #2 – Feedback from the Boss (Wrong Recipient)

Email from Scott Dunham <scott.d@flatcapco.com>
From: Scott Dunham <scott.d@flatcapco.com> To: You Sent: Monday, 4:17 PM Subject: Re: CapCast Draft Just saw your latest version. Not quite what I expected. Maybe rework a little? Something about the tone just isn’t landing. We might need to bring in someone else to punch it up if it doesn’t improve. I’ll be out this afternoon, but loop me in if you have a strong revision. —Scott
Context: You didn’t work on the CapCast Draft—this was likely sent to you by mistake.
Prompt: "Write a polite and professional reply that gently lets my boss know I didn’t work on that draft, and offer to help if needed."

Email #3 – Customer Reports Stolen Package

Email from terrythehatguy@gmail.com
From: terrythehatguy@gmail.com To: help@flatcapco.com Sent: Saturday, 6:12 PM Subject: Package issue Hey there, I’m not sure what the process is here, but according to tracking, my order was marked as delivered yesterday—but it wasn’t on my porch when I got home. I even checked with neighbors and nothing. This has never happened before and I’m kinda frustrated. It was a gift too. What am I supposed to do in this case? —Terry
Prompt: "Write a kind, professional reply acknowledging the missing package, explaining next steps, and showing empathy. Keep the tone supportive and clear."

Bonus: Build Your Writing Profile

After completing all three responses, give ChatGPT this prompt:

"Based on my edits and responses, create a writing profile that captures my tone, structure, and style preferences."

This will help you reuse your tone and voice in future AI-assisted writing tasks.
