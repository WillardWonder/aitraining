Flat Cap Co. – Media Campaign with AI

Example 3: Build a Full Campaign Through a Creative Conversation

This activity demonstrates how to use ChatGPT as a collaborative partner to generate promotional media—from visuals and music to social content and ad copy. This isn’t about pasting a single prompt. It’s about holding a conversation and turning AI-generated content into real marketing assets.

Step 1: Start the Conversation

Open ChatGPT and begin the conversation with:

Prompt:
"We want to host a flat cap–themed inflatable community night. Can you help me come up with posters, social media posts, and a radio ad to promote it? It should be fun, family-friendly, and energetic."

As ChatGPT responds, continue naturally by providing the event name, date, time, and location. You don’t need to script anything—just chat like you would with a creative teammate.

Step 2: Generate the Event Poster

After discussing the event, tell ChatGPT:

"Can you generate a promotional poster for this event, using flat caps as a theme and the event info?"

Then refine the visual by asking things like:

  • “Make it more colorful and retro.”
  • “Include the tagline ‘Hats Off to Summer!’”
  • “Add time and location at the bottom.”

Step 3: Create a Short Event Song

Continue the conversation with:

"Write a short, fun jingle or theme song for this event that could be used on the radio or social media."

Once you’re happy with the lyrics, you can turn them into a real audio track using AI song generators like Suno or Udio. Paste the lyrics in and choose a style (e.g., carnival, pop, or retro).

This demonstrates how ChatGPT's output can chain into another tool to create usable, shareable media.

Step 4: Build a Multi-Platform Social Campaign

Keep the momentum going by asking:

"Create a social media campaign for this event with custom posts for Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and Snapchat. Include hashtags and suggested post schedule."

Then refine with follow-ups like:

  • “Make Instagram more visual and add image suggestions.”
  • “Keep the Twitter version under 200 characters.”
  • “Add a playful teaser for Snapchat.”

Step 5: Write a 15-Second Radio Ad

Wrap the campaign with a final creative ask:

"Write a short 15-second radio ad that’s energetic, repeats ‘Flat Cap Co.’ at least twice, and ends with the tagline ‘Hats Off to Summer!’"

Ask for tone adjustments, rhyming versions, or announcer-style reads to match your event energy.

Key Takeaways

  • This is a full, progressive conversation—not a script.
  • Each step builds on the last. Let ChatGPT help you shape and refine the campaign.
  • When the AI generates lyrics, content, or images—you can send that output into other AI tools to create real media.
  • Always think: “Would I actually use this?” If not, keep the conversation going.
